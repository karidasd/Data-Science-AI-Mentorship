# 📧 Cold Message Templates that actually work

Applying through the company portal is a black hole. The best way to get an interview is to send a "Cold Message" to the Hiring Manager or Senior Data Scientist on LinkedIn.

Here are tested templates you can copy, paste, and customize.

---

## Template 1: The "Portfolio Drop"
*Use this when messaging a Senior Data Scientist or Manager at a company you admire.*

**Subject:** Quick question regarding the Data team at [Company Name]

Hi [Name],

I saw you are a [Job Title] at [Company Name]. I've been following the company's work on [Mention something specific, e.g., your recent blog post about predictive modeling], and I love your approach.

I am currently a Junior Data Scientist and I just finished building an End-to-End project that uses [Technology they use, e.g., PySpark and AWS] to predict [X]. 

If you have 60 seconds, I'd love your brutally honest feedback on the architecture of my GitHub repo: [Link to Repo].

No worries if you are too busy. Keep up the great work!

Best,
[Your Name]

---

## Template 2: The "Direct Approach" (Recruiters)
*Use this for Tech Recruiters after you have applied online.*

Hi [Recruiter Name],

I just applied for the [Job Title] position at [Company]. 

I know you receive hundreds of applications, so I wanted to quickly highlight that I have hands-on experience with the exact stack you are looking for ([Skill 1], [Skill 2], and [Skill 3]), which I recently used to build [Link to Portfolio Project].

I would love to chat for 5 minutes if my profile aligns with what the team needs. 

Best,
[Your Name]

---

## Template 3: The "Informational Interview"
*Use this when you just want to build a network and ask for a 15-minute virtual coffee.*

Hi [Name],

I'm a huge fan of the data engineering architecture you've built at [Company]. I'm currently transitioning into Data Engineering and I'm highly focused on mastering dbt and Airflow.

I am not messaging to ask for a job. I'd just love to pick your brain for 15 minutes over a virtual coffee about how you scaled your pipelines. 

Would you be open to a quick chat next week?

Best,
[Your Name]

---

## Template 4: The "Alumni Connection"
*Use this when someone went to your University or Bootcamp.*

Hi [Name],

I saw you also graduated from [University/Bootcamp Name] - go [Mascot]! 

I'm currently looking to transition into a [Job Role] role, and seeing your trajectory at [Company] is incredibly inspiring. I'd love to ask you a couple of quick questions about your transition from academia to industry.

Do you have 10 minutes for a quick chat later this week?

Best,
[Your Name]

---

## Template 5: The "Polite Follow-up"
*Use this when they ghost you after 4-5 days.*

Hi [Name],

Bringing this to the top of your inbox. I know things get incredibly busy on the data team! 

If you don't have time right now, no problem at all. I will keep following your work. 

Thanks,
[Your Name]
